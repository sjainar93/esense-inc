What is eSense?

eSense Defined:
eSense is a sense of extraordinary commitment to adding value for our clients and employees in everything we do.
It is the comforting constant that harmonizes technology and business environments where change is the only other constant.
It is a set of service-oriented principles rooted in expertise and common sense.
eSense is not just our identity it’s in our name.

Our Guiding Principles

  • People can function without technology, but technology is useless without people.
  • Always put people first technology follows.
  • Continuously focus on delivering value to our clients.
  • Empower every team member to make and own decisions with accountability.
  • Encourage self-motivation, reliability, and both independence and interdependence.
  • Deliver iterative, incremental value throughout every project lifecycle.
  • Bridge the understanding between technical and business teams.
  • Favor implemented processes over documented processes — execution over theory.
  • Adapt processes practically to fit client culture, not just industry templates.
  • Remember: We are in the service industry. Technology is only a tool to serve our clients.
  • Go paperless — for efficient communication and a sustainable planet.

At eSense, our culture is our commitment — to people, performance, and purpose.
