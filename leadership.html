He excels in managing cash flow, translating strategy into execution, and turning around financially challenged companies.

Expertise:

  • Software Selection & Implementation
  • Loan Negotiation & Banking Relations
  • Strategic Planning & Budget Oversight
  • Employee Engagement, Insurance & Retirement

He has a background in the construction industry, with tons of insight with companies competing in a service industry.
